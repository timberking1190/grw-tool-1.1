🧬
Facial Action Coding System (FACS)

Developed by Dr. Paul Ekman — the world's foremost expert on facial expressions and deception — FACS maps 44 individual facial muscle movements (Action Units) and their combinations into precisely classified emotional and cognitive states. It's the same system used by Pixar to animate human faces, the US Department of Defense to detect deception, and elite sports psychologists globally.

Ekman, P. (1978) — University of California
🧍
Kinesics & Postural Analysis

Ray Birdwhistell's kinesics research established that 70% of human communication is nonverbal. Boost Intelligence classifies 120+ postural and gestural signals — power stance indicators, submission clusters, engagement postures, and self-soothing behaviors — to build a complete behavioral fingerprint of every subject in your footage.

Birdwhistell (1970) — Kinetics & Context
🧠
Attentional Blink & Flow State Detection

Using gaze tracking proxies and blink-rate variance, we identify peak cognitive performance windows — including flow state entry and exit — and attentional disruption events. Based on Csikszentmihalyi's Flow theory and Shapiro's attentional blink research. Tells you not just where someone is, but where their mind is.

Csikszentmihalyi (1990) — Flow Theory
🔗
Group Dynamics & Social Synchrony

Interpersonal synchrony research shows that mirroring, turn-taking, and gestural alignment predict team cohesion with 83% accuracy. Our group analysis mode maps dominance hierarchies, coalition formation, and collective buy-in signals across everyone in your footage simultaneously — making the invisible dynamics in your locker room or boardroom visible.

Lakens (2010) — Psychological Science
